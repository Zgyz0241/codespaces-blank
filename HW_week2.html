Snack Street

Every summer night, as the sun disappears from the horizon and the heat on the ground slowly recedes, the Snack Street begins to come alive. People converge from all corners of the city and they always arrive on time, as if this were a ritual, an unwritten appointment they had with summer.

Barbecue

I walked on the asphalt road, the air was filled with the tantalizing scent of grilled meats, spices and marinades...

Cold Snacks

However, out of all the hustle, it was the cold snacks that made me happiest. A sip of cold drink on a hot summer day is not to be missed...

Night Market

But the snack street wasn't all about food. We affectionately called it the "night market," a place where one could find everything from quirky ornaments to rare collectibles...

Memory

  • Adults leisurely enjoy their BBQ with old friends.
  • Children racing around with ice-cold treats dripping down their hands.

    • At this moment, everyone is soaking in the magic of a warm summer night. Those nights, with their rich tapestry of experiences, are forever etched in my memory, serving as a testament to the simple joys of life.
